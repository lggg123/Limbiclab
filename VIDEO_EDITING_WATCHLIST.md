## Legal & Content Flags

### Audio to mute or bleep
- Russian Satanist clip: she said "fuck me" — **bleep or cut the word**, keep the reaction
- Any full names spoken on camera by strangers — mute or blur
- Business names in background signage that could imply endorsement — not critical but be aware

### Privacy (California public filming law)
- You're clear on all outdoor public space footage — no consent required
- Indoor private venues (bars, cafes): if staff asked you to stop, that footage is risky
- Minors on camera: blur faces even in public

### Music
- Do NOT use any copyrighted music even as background — YouTube will mute or strike the video
- Use YouTube Audio Library or license-free tracks only
- If you want dark ambient/drone music, search: "dark ambient no copyright" on YouTube Audio Library

## CapCut Edit Checklist

### Format
- [ ] Export 1920×1080 (16:9 horizontal)
- [ ] 30fps minimum, 60fps if your footage supports it
- [ ] Bitrate: High (for YouTube compression headroom)

### Pacing
- Church footage: slow, let it breathe — 0.5x or normal speed
- Interview clips: cut tight, remove dead air and filler words ("um", "like")
- Hollywood/street: faster cuts, energy up
- Playa Del Rey: use it as a transition beat between heavier segments

### Text overlays
- Font: monospace (matches LimbicLab brand)
- Color: white text on dark background or teal `#2a9d9d` accent
- Keep it minimal — one line at a time, max 5 words
- Bottom third placement for captions, center for title cards

### Audio levels
- Voice: -12dB to -6dB (clear and present)
- Background music: -20dB to -18dB (underneath, not competing)
- Check on phone speaker — most viewers watch on mobile

### Transitions
- Hard cuts for intensity/dark comedy beats
- 0.3–0.5 sec fade for scene changes (church → street)
- Avoid wipes/zooms — they look cheap

## Before You Export — Final QC

- [ ] Watch full video with headphones — catch audio issues
- [ ] Watch on phone screen — check text readability
- [ ] No jump cuts that feel accidental (only intentional ones)
- [ ] Censored audio is clean — bleep is timed correctly
- [ ] End card CTA text is readable in under 3 seconds
- [ ] Color grade consistent across clips (church vs street lighting will differ)
- [ ] No dead silence longer than 1 second unless intentional
- [ ] Total runtime: 3–5 minutes minimum for YouTube ad eligibility

## Color Grading Notes
Church and indoor footage will be warmer/yellower.  
Street and Hollywood footage will be cooler/bluer at night.  
In CapCut: pull highlights down, push shadows slightly, desaturate toward teal/dark palette to match LimbicLab aesthetic.
