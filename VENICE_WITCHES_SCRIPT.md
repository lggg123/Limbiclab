# The Witches of Venice — Dark Comedy Script

> Hook D · Target: Wellness / Spirituality Audience · Location: Abbott Kinney & Venice Boardwalk, CA

---

## Overview

Dark comedy angle targeting the $1.8T wellness industry audience — crystals, tarot, ritual. Frames LimbicLab as the neuroscience explanation behind what the witch already knows. Runs as Hook D in the YouTube ads campaign alongside Hook A (church), Hook B (Russian Satanist), Hook C (Hollywood command).

**Tone**: Deadpan. Respectful of the witch. Funny because it's true.

## Delivery Notes

- **Mercury section** is the funniest — slow down on *"minding its business"*
- **"Karen in Santa Monica"** — deadpan, no smile
- **The closer** — genuine, not sarcastic. The witch bit lands because you mean it
- Record 2–3 takes per act, pick the one where you sound least like you're reading
